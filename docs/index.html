Marine Design Technology Consulting


Who are we?

We are a new consulting firm specializing in technology solutions for the marine design and shipbuilding industries. We have worked on all kinds of ships, civil and military, from tugboats to submarines, from creating the 3D model of the ships, to drafting productions outputs, to setting up project databases and standards.


What do we do?

The design lifecycle of a ship is long and complex, and our mission is to streamline and automate processes wherever possible. By leveraging advanced technology, we enhance efficiency, empower engineers and designers, and deliver more precise outcomes. As ship designs constantly evolve, we offer tools to ensure every design change is seamlessly implemented through to production.

Our expertise in FORAN allows us to install and set up the system in a new design office or shipyard based on their needs and processes, and to maintain the configurations and settings, providing assistance and training to the clients to ensure a smooth transition through the system implementation.

We also provide tools to add extra value to the system like custom automated reporting, 3D exports, and drawing batch processing.
